2. Morning Routine

Start your day by marking your attendance on time.

☀️ Check-In Window

08:00 AM - 11:59 AM

Step-by-Step

1 Ensure "Location" says Verified.

2 Locate the "Morning Check-In" card.

3 Tap the big CHECK IN button.

4 Confirm the popup action.

Success Indicators:

  • Green Tick Mark ✓ appears
  • Punch time is recorded (e.g., "09:05 AM")
  • Status updates to ON DUTY
🚫
Missed the Window?
If you do not check in by 11:59 AM, the Morning slot will lock. You will be marked Absent for the morning.

3. Afternoon & End of Day

🌤️ Afternoon (Optional)

12:00 PM - 02:59 PM • Only for abrupt status changes.

↓ ENTERS

Use if you missed Morning Check-in and are arriving late (After 12 PM).

↑ LEAVES

Use if you are leaving early for the day (Half Day).

Leave as "NONE" for a normal working day.

🌙 Evening Check-Out

03:00 PM - 11:30 PM • Mandatory for Full Day.

Check-Out Process

1 Verify you are at the office.

2 Tap CHECK OUT on the Evening card.

3 App confirms with "COMPLETED" status.

💡
Why Check Out?
You must check out to mark the day as "Full Day". If you forget, it may be counted as a missed punch.

4. Troubleshooting

Location Issues

📍
GPS / Location Error
"Outside Range" or "Unverified"
  • Solution 1: Ensure your GPS is ON.
  • Solution 2: Open Google Maps to calibrate your location, then return.
  • Solution 3: Ensure you are physically within the office premises.

Security Warnings

🛡️
Critical Security Alert
"Security Issue Detected"

The app has strict security. This error appears if:

  • You are using a "Fake GPS" or "Mock Location" app. Uninstall immediately.
  • Your device is rooted or compromised.

The app will not work until unauthorized tools are removed.

Internet & Sync

Error: "Sync Error" or "Network Error"

  • Check Mobile Data / Wi-Fi.
  • Pull down on the screen to Refresh.

Need Help?

Tap the Blue Chat Button ● to access the Issue Reporting System.

Use this to report app crashes, location errors, or attendance corrections.
